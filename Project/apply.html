HOW TO BECOME A MEMBER?


Timeline: The 4 "I"s


  • STEP 1.
    INFORMATION SESSION

    Attend a recruitment information session in Fall or Winter quarter! We will be hosting our information sessions on the University of Washington Seattle campus.

    Mark the date. Like AIESEC Seattle on Facebook or check on AIESEC Seattle's website's to find out when is the next information session.

    Winter Recruitment Info Sessions: 

    Jan 20th, 2015 | 6 - 7pm | @ Dempsey Hall 124

    Jan 21st, 2015 | 6 - 7pm | @ Dempsey Hall 124

    If you want to be a member but cannot make it to the information session or have any question, please
    contact us.

  • STEP 2.
    I APPLY

    Complete your application carefully and thoughtfully.
    After attending your first information session, you will fill out your application here, and select "University of Washington."

    You will be notified via email on your status.

  • STEP 3.
    INTERVIEWS

    Interview day will consist of interviews and assessment.
    We will send out an invitation letter for both the assessment day and interviews after screening the application. Check the dates in your e-mail and on Facebook event page.

    YAfter final evaluation of your application and assessment, we will notify you on your status via email.

  • STEP 4.
    IMPACT

    Congratulations - Welcome to AIESEC Seattle!
    You are now a part of the global network of over one million youth leaders. Get ready to develop yourself to become a
    global leader and be ready to make some impact!!!
